# Formula 1 2016: Rosberg vs Hamilton Champinsnhip Battle

**OVERVIEW**
This Power BI dashboard explores one of the most dramatic Formula 1 seasons in modern history, the 2016 championship battle between Nico Rosberg and Lewis Hamilton.
Using race results data, the dashboard compares wins, podium finishes, reliability, qualifying conversion, and overall chmapionship progression throughout the season.

**OBJECTIVES**
- Compare the season-long performance of the two title contenders
- Analyze race wins and podium consistency
- Evaluate reliability and retirements during the title fight
- Explore how qualifying positions translated into race finishes
- Provide broader context through driver and constructor standings

**KEY INSIGHTS**
- The 2016 championship was decided by only 5 points, making it one of the closest little battles of the era.
- Lewis Hamilton led in race wins and podium Finishes
- Nico Rosberg secured the championship through consistency and crucial race results
- Reliability and finishing every opportunity played a major role in the final standings
- The Mercedes team dominated the season while hosting an internal championship fight
